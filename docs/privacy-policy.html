Privacy Policy

Motion Trails · Last updated 27 June 2026

Motion Trails does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. Everything the app does happens entirely on your device.

Your videos stay on your device

Clips you record or import, the previews and trail effects the app generates, and the videos you export are processed and stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. They are never uploaded to us or to any third party. Motion Trails has no server component and makes no network requests with your content.

Camera and Photos

The app asks for camera access only to record short clips for the trail effect, and for Photos access only to import clips you choose and to save your finished videos back to your photo library. These permissions are used solely on-device, at your request. You can change them at any time in iOS Settings → Motion Trails.

Data we collect

None. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, location, contacts, or any other personal information. The app stores your projects and settings only in its own local storage on your device.

Children’s privacy

Motion Trails is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
